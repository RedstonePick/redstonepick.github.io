About


Goals

The Goals of this site is for both osu!taiko mappers and players to be able to find maps to play and create.
As a player you can find beatmaps you can play with our enhanced search.
As a mapper you can find new things to map, find influence, and get paid for making a map.


Why I created this


The osu!taiko community feels small but we are a pretty decent size group.\
I created this because I felt that the amount of maps we get seems small and the playerbase seems mad at a decent amount.
This website is some sort of "mapping bounty" website with the goal of
if you want a map for something you can find a good mapper to map and rank it.
Having an incentive to map will bring in more mappers and a larger collection of maps for those who just got into taiko.
